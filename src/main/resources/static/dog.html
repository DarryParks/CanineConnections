Different Types of Dogs and Skill Sets

Bulldog

The Bulldog is a medium-sized breed known for its loose, wrinkled skin and distinctive pushed-in nose.

  • Guarding
  • Obedience

Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is a friendly and versatile breed known for its intelligence and willingness to please.

  • Retrieving
  • Assistance Work

German Shepherd

The German Shepherd is a highly intelligent and loyal breed often used in working roles such as police and service dogs.

  • Tracking
  • Protection Work

Poodle

The Poodle is a highly intelligent and elegant breed known for its curly coat and versatility in various dog sports.

  • Agility
  • Obedience

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a friendly and gentle breed that is often used as a therapy or assistance dog due to its calm and reliable nature.

  • Therapy Work
  • Search and Rescue
